    INTRODUCTION

    3E Academy was founded by Rukhsar Ali, A student at that time enrolled in BS Software Engineering Program but had good skills in English Reading, Writing and Speaking.
    3E Academy currently is for Mirpur Khas (A city of Sindh Province of Pakistan) only with a vision to enhance it's branches throughout the country.
    3E Academy's main objective is to teach students in a better way as soon as possible so that their money is not wasted more than it should.
    3E Academy hopes to make you Ethical, Ellegiant and Efficient.
